Olivia Benson

Olivia Benson, born February 7, 1968, is a child of her mother's rape. Benson has a younger half-brother named Simon Marsden, whom she found by illegally running her DNA through the system. Benson is a graduate of Siena College. In addition to English, Benson speaks Italian, Russian, some Spanish and French. At the conclusion of season 15 finale, Benson becomes the court-appointed custodial guardian of Noah Porter, an orphaned baby. The appointment is for a trial period of one year, but Benson grows to love Noah and formally adopts him. Benson is tough but empathetic, getting emotionally involved in cases.Executive producer and head writer Neal Baer has explained that she is "the empathetic, passionate voice for these victims", in contrast to Stabler, who embodies "the rage we feel, the 'How can this happen?' feeling". During the 15th season, Benson is promoted to sergeant and appointed as squad supervisor. In Season 17, she is promoted to Lieutenant and becomes the squad's official commanding officer. She is promoted to Captain in the 21st season.
